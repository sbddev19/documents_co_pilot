Implementation Checklist

Data & Events
  • Stream transactions to Kafka/Flink; derive event signals in near‑real‑time.
  • Persist features in a shared Feature Store; refresh incremental features.
Models & CSI
  • Train per‑product propensity models; calibrate thresholds per market.
  • Compute CSI (0–100) and cache in Redis for <100ms RTIM reads.
  • Enable XAI “Why this offer?” snippets.
RTIM + AEM
  • Map events→offers in RTIM; enforce frequency caps & fatigue rules.
  • In AEM, tag content by persona (Gen‑Z) + CSI tier + market locale.
Go‑Live & Measurement
  • A/B test CSI‑driven journeys vs rule‑based control.
  • Track conversion, latency, engagement; retrain weekly.
