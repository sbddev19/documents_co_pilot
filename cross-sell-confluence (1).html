1) Strategic Vision

Evolve from “pushing products” to a Personalized Financial Journey Companion that anticipates needs and recommends the next best action (NBA) and next best offer (NBO) aligned to local norms.

3) Signals → Opportunities (by Region)

Singapore Wealth‑Building

  • Salary inflow (≥ SGD 8–10k) → Robo/ETF portfolios, unit trusts
  • High CPF but low investable cash → Supplementary retirement plans
  • Family‑linked accounts → Family term/health insurance
  • Frequent PayNow/Grab → Lifestyle rewards cards

Hong Kong Multi‑Currency Lifestyle

  • Remittance & FX usage → Low‑fee multicurrency account, instant FX
  • Large cash balance → Tiered time deposits
  • Trading activity → Wealth platform upsell (equities/ETFs)
  • High dining/travel spend → Premium card + lounge access

Europe Trust & Sustainability

  • Steady income (25–35) → ESG/low‑risk funds
  • Salary + rent → Home insurance or savings‑linked mortgage
  • Debit‑heavy spend → Green/cashback credit card
  • Frequent EU travel → Virtual multicurrency card + insurance

5) Implementation Framework

  1. Unify data (Customer 360): identity resolution, feature store, consent ledger.
  2. Models: Affinity, Propensity, Risk, Uplift; champion‑challenger setup.
  3. Event‑driven triggers: salary credited, high‑value txn, travel‑spend spikes.
  4. Decisioning: eligibility, fairness, caps, “Why this offer?” explanations.
  5. Omnichannel: in‑app cards, push, email, WhatsApp (SG/HK), web (EU).
  6. Observability: dashboards for CTR, CVR, revenue, and fairness metrics.

8) Compliance & Governance

  • Singapore (MAS): Fair Dealing, MyInfo consent, SGFinDex data minimization.
  • Hong Kong (HKMA): Open API security profiles, transparent FX disclosures.
  • Europe (GDPR/PSD2): explicit opt‑in for profiling, DPIA, Right to Explanation, SCA.
  • Guardrails: frequency caps, exclude vulnerable cohorts, audit logs for offers.
