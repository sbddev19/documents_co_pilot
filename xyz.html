Key Assumptions

  • Migration covers mobile CMS content into Adobe Experience Manager for headless or mobile content delivery.
  • Majority of the effort is assigned to AEM development, frontend integration and testing.
  • Existing mobile APIs are mostly reusable with limited changes.
  • AEM environments and CI/CD pipelines are already available.
  • Content authors and business users participate in UAT and content validation.
  • Release manager supports deployment planning, cutover, rollback and release governance.
